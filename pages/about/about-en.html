About us

What we do

Queer Squad is a Chinese-speaking queer community based in Frankfurt am Main (and also everywhere in Germany), focusing on LGBTQ+, feminism, diasporic queerness and anti-racism with interdisciplinary aspects such as moving-image, art, (sub)culture and activism.

Our film screenings, panel talks, workshops and community meetups are devoted to creating a public (safe) space for LGBTQ+ Chinese diasporas to discuss gender/sexuality, trans-cultural identities and anti-racist topics in the socio-political context. Let's embrace Queerness!
